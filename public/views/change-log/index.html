19th June 2017

Combat Statistics Update

Having been absent from the website for quite some time now (and was on previous versions), I've added the Combat Statistics section, available on the home page under COMBAT.

This section shows the overall combat statistics (kills, deaths etc) and displays not only the total but the faction distribution. This way you're able to see exactly how many kills a VS Heavy Assault got in an alert.

The section also shows the comparisons across classes, e.g. Heavy Assault kills vs Infiltrator kills.

9th June 2017

Alert Real Time Update!

After a year long hiatus - and unfortunately being unemployed :-/ - I've taken some time to develop my flagship website.

Interested in knowing who's at the top of the leaderboard at any given moment in an alert? Now you'll know!

To that effect, I've now completed the real time mechanics on the alert pages. That means, every kill, death, map capture etc will be reflected on the result page in real time whenever an alert is in progress.

I've also taken the opportunity to fix various bugs, layout issues etc and giving the website general polish. If you have any suggestions or bug reports, let me know on the GitHub page!

I've also updated the project page, reflecting the project's current progress and what I plan to work on.

19th June 2016

Homepage Update & Project Statuses consolidated

Victory Timeline

This handy graph shows the victories for each faction over time, allowing you to see the trends that are occuring. This graph will be improved over time, with labels detailing when major patches landed so you can see their affect on alerts coming soon.

Victory Breakdowns

There's now 4 new statistics panels which show you how each server's victories are distributed. E.g. You're now able to see which servers the Vanu Soverignity are doing best on Indar. (As of writing, that's Miller and Cobalt!).

Global Server Victories - Victories broken down by each server, showing how each faction are doing on each server.

Global Continent Victories - Victories broken down by continent, showing which faction is dominating Indar! (As of writing, that's the TR!)

Per-Server Continental Victories - Victories broken down by Server and by Continent, which allows you see how each continent fairs for each faction on each server.

Faction Victory Contributions - Shows a breakdown of the total number of wins, per faction, and how they contribute to the overall faction percentages. Shown in both a segmented bar chart and in tabular form. Interested to see which servers are pulling their weight? Now you can find out!

3rd May 2016

Leaderboards

I'm happy to announce the new Leaderboard system! While in it's initial version, you'll be able to see the top 100 players, outfits and weapons by various metrics. See who are the top farmers, teamkillers, suiciders and crops, by all servers or by single servers.

This version is the start of something quite big in PS2. The website will soon™ have competitive ladders, which will pit you against other players of your server, and globally, and give you a ranking. More on this soon.

29th April 2016

Outfit Profiles

Outfit profiles are now live! Wonder how you compare to the rest of your outfit mates? Now you can find out!

The outfit profiles show stats such as:

  • Kills, Deaths, Teamkills, Suicides and headshots across all alerts recorded
  • Averages, such as K/D, Kills & Deaths per alert etc
  • Facility Statistics, along with all counts of captures and defences for every facility for that outfit
  • A list of all alerts that the outfit has participated in

24th April 2016

Player Profiles

I'm happy to present the first iteration of Player Profiles! With player profiles, you'll be able to see the statistics for any particular player we have on record, including stats such as:

  • Kills, Deaths, Teamkills, Suicides and headshots across all alerts recorded
  • Averages, such as K/D, Kills & Deaths per alert etc
  • A list of all alerts that a player has participated in
  • All weapon kills, headshots and teamkills a player has accumulated
  • All vehicle metrics - note, that this metric is a bit dodgy and may be removed

Change Log - As part of this update, a Change Log page has also been added, allowing you to see a record of what's changed over time, rather than camming everything into this small announcement box!
