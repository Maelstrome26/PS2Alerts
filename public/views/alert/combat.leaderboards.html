There is a small chance that headshots get miscalculated in a way where you have more headshots than kills. This is a bug that's been plauging the system for years, and there's no known fix at this time. It's pretty rare.
* Outfits that have less than 5 players in an alert during "Prime Time" will have their K/Ds zeroed, in order to prevent one player from having a high K/D and polluting the outfit stats. This won't affect sitewide metrics, this is purely for visual representation on a per-alert basis.
  • [I] = Infrantry Weapons
  • [V] = Vehicle Weapons
  • Weapons that are available to all factions (such as the Frag Grenade) are listed as (Grouped), where all their stats are tallied together. They are also listed seperately so you can see a faction breakdown.
  • NS Infrantry weapons are regarded as one weapon to the game, so they're automatically grouped together. Faction information for these weapons is not possible currently.
  • Certain weapons are incapable of headshots (such as the frag grenade). Showing 0% is intended.
  • * Infrantry Death calculations are currently broken. Possibly they're being regarded as vehicle deaths by mistake. Sorry!
